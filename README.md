# SE_DAY4_Software-Project-Management
## 1. Why is timely delivery crucial in software project management, and how can project managers ensure that deadlines are met?
Timely delivery ensures client satisfaction and saves money. Project manager can ensure deadlines are met by creating clear and realistic plans and ensuring effective communication among the members.
## 2. How does effective cost control contribute to the success of a software project? What strategies can be used to prevent budget overruns?
Cost Control ensures profitability by minimizing unecessary expenses. Effective scope management can be used to prevent budget overruns by ensuring the initial scope is prioritized
## 3. Compare and contrast Agile and Waterfall methodologies. What are the main advantages and disadvantages of each?
Agile methodology involves development in sprints which focus on a particular tasks while Waterfall involves moving through the SDLC process one step at a time
Main Advantage of Agile is Flexibility meaning that it can adapt to changes in requirements
Main Disadvantage of Agile is Less predictable cost meaning that it is hard to estimate budgets and timelines
Main Advantage of WaterFall is clear structure meaning phases are well defined making execution and planning straight forward
Main Disadvantage of WaterFall is inflexibility meaning it is difficult to implement once development begins
## 4. In what types of projects might Agile be more beneficial than Waterfall, and vice versa? Can you provide examples of each?
Agile is benefitial for projects with evolving requirement and those in which customer feedback is important. An example is web development project where constinous user feedback is required.
Waterfall is benefitial for projects with well defined requirement and minimal changes are required. An example is an ERP system development with predefined workflows and configurations
## 5. What are some methods for ensuring quality assurance throughout a software project? Why is it important to maintain high standards?
Some of the strategies in ensuring quality include testing and clear requirements gathering. It is important to maintain high standards in order to have a competitive advantage and ensure user satisfaction
## 6. How does defining the project scope contribute to successful project planning? What is a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS), and why is it useful?
Defining scope establishes clear boundaries and objectives fpor a project. WBS is an hierichical decomposition of a project into smaller manageable components. WBS is useful because it improves planning and estimation and defines scope clearly.
## 7. What are the benefits of developing a detailed project schedule, and how can Gantt charts assist in this process?
Benefits of developing a detailed project schedule include improved time management, clear roadmap and progress tracking. Gantt chart assist in project schedule development by displaying tasks, their dependencies and duration in a timeline format
